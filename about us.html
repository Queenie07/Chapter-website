ABOUT US


IEEE-Computer Society is a student chapter in VIT, Vellore.
Founded in 2012 by a group of tech enthusiasts, the chapter has been on an amazing journey ever since!
Our chapter's mission is to foster innovation, learning and networking in the most happening areas of computing technologies and we have been doing so by conducting various workshops and events throughout the semester over the years.

Here, at IEEE-Computer Society, we believe that regardless of one's stream and interests, everyone can learn something from one another. We value a person's curiosity as much as we value knowledge. The exciting projects that our team members work on is sure to gratify the geek in you!
